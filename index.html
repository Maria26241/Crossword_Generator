Start-up Instructions (Adjusts once you press "Start!")

  • INPUT a word-hint combination, separated by a space.
    Example: cow An animal that moos.
  • Press "Add" to add your input to the list
  • Press "Delete" to remove the last list item if the input is empty, or input the exact word, hint or both then press "Delete" to remove that selection.
  • Repetitions of words and/or hints are prohibited.
  • Press "Start" once you have your list ready. You may not edit this list after pressing "Start".
  • A crossword will be generated and you'll be able to play!
